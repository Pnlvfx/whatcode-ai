# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 3, 2026_

WhatCode ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What we collect

**Push notification token** - When you enable notifications, your device's APNs token is sent to our relay server. This token is used solely to deliver push notifications to your device and is never shared with third parties.

**Machine ID** - A unique identifier for the machine running the WhatCode daemon is generated locally and used to distinguish connections in the app. It is not linked to your identity.

**No account required** - WhatCode does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.

**No analytics** - We do not use any analytics SDKs or third-party tracking in the iOS app.

## Your code and data

WhatCode connects directly to the OpenCode server running on your own machine. Your code, conversations, and project data never pass through our servers. The relay server only receives and forwards push notification payloads - it never sees your code or session content.

## Data retention

APNs tokens are stored only as long as your account is active. If you remove the app or disconnect an account, the token is no longer used.

## Third-party services

WhatCode uses **Apple Push Notification service (APNs)** to deliver notifications. Apple's privacy policy applies to data handled by APNs.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
